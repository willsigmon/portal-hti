The Collaboration

HUBZone Technology Initiative

HTI is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit bridging the digital divide by transforming retired laptops into secure Chromebooks and providing digital literacy training to NC families who lack access to technology.

  • Secure US DoD-grade data wipes on all devices
  • Complete hardware refurbishment and setup
  • Free distributions and literacy courses
Visit hubzonetech.org ➔

Portal HQ

Raleigh’s premier community workspace and multi-purpose venue. Fostering a high-energy creative ecosystem for entrepreneurs, artists, and creators to connect and execute.

  • Professional concert AV, sound, and lighting
  • Fully adaptable 5,000 sq ft creative hub
  • Focused on elevating NC founders
Visit theportalhq.com ➔
OUR REAL MISSION

Close the Digital Divide

For thousands of kids in North Carolina, a laptop isn't just a screen—it's their only doorway to the future. Without one, doing homework, discovering new talents, or dreaming of a better tomorrow is nearly impossible.

By bringing your old laptop to our social hour, you aren't just recycling technology. You are giving a local student the key to their education, their passions, and their wildest dreams. Let's make sure no child gets left in the dark.
